# Discovery Hub: The Power of Open-Source

DepthSight is a fully **Open-Source** platform. Our core philosophy is that your funds and API keys should always remain under your absolute control. Anyone can deploy the platform on their own server (Self-Hosted), maintaining maximum privacy and security.

At the same time, the **Discovery Hub** connects all independent user servers (nodes) into a single global network. Even while trading from your private server, you remain part of a massive community: you can find ready-made algorithms, share profitable ideas, and interact with traders from all over the world.

The section consists of four main tabs.

---

## 1. Verified Templates
This tab contains official strategies created and thoroughly tested by the DepthSight team.

*   **Why use it**: Perfect for beginners. You don't need to build a strategy from scratch—just pick a suitable template (e.g., trend scalping or mean reversion).
*   **How to use**: Click the **Import Config** button. The strategy will be automatically copied to your personal library, and you can open it in the Strategy Editor to tweak the parameters to your liking.

## 2. Trading Ideas
Here you will find algorithms shared by other users of the platform. It's similar to "Ideas" on TradingView, but specifically for algorithmic trading.

*   **Analysis**: On each card, you can see the actual backtest or live trading metrics of that strategy (PnL, number of trades, drawdown).
*   **Voting**: You can vote for the best ideas (Upvote). The most profitable and popular strategies rise to the top.
*   **Copying (Inspect)**: If you like the results of someone else's strategy, click **Inspect**. You will be able to study in detail which blocks it consists of, how the filters and risk management are configured, and then save it for yourself.

## 3. Discussions
An internal forum within the platform.
*   **What to write about**: Ask questions about block configurations, discuss the current market phase with the Oracle, or share your insights on new indicators.
*   **Features**: You can create new topics, leave comments, and attach your strategy configurations directly in your messages.

## 4. Network Status
A visualization of DepthSight's global infrastructure and the strength of our Open-Source community.
*   **What it shows**: A map of active platform nodes (servers) around the world (Frankfurt, New York, Tokyo, etc.), their ping to exchanges, and uptime.
*   **Why use it**: DepthSight is an open platform. This tab demonstrates that anyone can deploy their **own node (Self-Hosted)** on their server. You can trade and keep your API keys completely secure on your own hardware, while still being a fully-fledged member of the global community, sharing strategies and statistics through the shared network.

---

### Pro Tip:
Don't be afraid to share your strategies in the **Trading Ideas** tab. The community might point out logic errors you missed (e.g., "over-optimization" or a missing time filter) and help you improve your algorithm!
